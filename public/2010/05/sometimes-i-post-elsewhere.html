Sometimes, I post elsewhere

2010-05-17 Monday

This blog is about code, programming, developer collaboration and my technology woes. I have another blog where I talk about pretty much everything else that I find interesting.

They are split up because a lot of the readers of Echo and Bounce have no interest and little understanding of the minutiae of Python hacking, and because Mere Code is syndicated out to stacks of planets where the readers probably don’t care about my thoughts on Puritans and business yoga probloggers, or why I think garbage bins are so great.

If you do find that sort of stuff interesting, I warmly invite you to subscribe to my other blog, Echo and Bounce, or to my combined RSS feed.
